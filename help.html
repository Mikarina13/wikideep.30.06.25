Publishing AI Content

Share your valuable AI-generated insights with the community and help build the world's largest open AI knowledge archive.

Archive Posts

üìö How to Publish Archive Content:

  1. Click "Publish" in the navigation menu
  2. Choose "Archive" tab (or it will auto-select based on your page)
  3. Fill in the details:
    • Title: Descriptive title for your insight
    • AI Model: Which AI generated this content
    • Original Prompt: Your prompt (can be kept private)
    • Content: Paste the AI response OR share a link
    • Tags: Add relevant keywords for discovery
  4. Choose content type: Paste text directly or share a link (Google Docs, ChatGPT conversations, etc.)
  5. Set prompt privacy: Share your prompt publicly or keep it private
  6. Publish! Your content joins the open archive
Pro Tip:

High-quality, detailed AI responses work best! Think research summaries, complex explanations, creative outputs, or problem-solving results that took significant prompting.

Collaboration Posts

ü§ù How to Post Collaboration Opportunities:

  1. Click "Publish" and select collaboration type
  2. Choose your type: "Looking for Collaboration" or "Offering to Collaborate"
  3. Write a compelling title that clearly describes your project or skills
  4. Detailed description: Explain your project, goals, timeline, and what you're looking for
  5. Add relevant tags: Skills, technologies, project types, experience level
  6. Provide contact info: Your email for interested collaborators
  7. Publish! Your opportunity appears in the collaboration board

Browsing & Discovery

Finding Content

  • Search: Use the search bar to find specific topics, keywords, or AI models
  • Filter by AI Model: Browse content from specific AI models (GPT-4, Claude, Gemini, etc.)
  • Sort Options: Newest, oldest, most viewed, alphabetical, or most favorited
  • Tag Browsing: Click on tags to discover related content
  • Advanced Filters: Filter by content type, prompt visibility, date range, view count

Favorites & Activity

  • Save Favorites: Click the heart icon on any post to save it to your favorites
  • Download Content: Download archive posts as text files for offline reading
  • Track Activity: View your browsing history and recently visited posts
  • Manage Collections: Organize your favorites by type (archive vs collaboration)

Best Practices & Tips

Quality Content Guidelines

  • Be Descriptive: Use clear, specific titles that accurately describe your content
  • Add Context: Include the AI model used and generation date when possible
  • Tag Thoughtfully: Use relevant, specific tags that help others discover your content
  • Share Prompts: When comfortable, share your prompts to help others learn
  • Verify Accuracy: Review AI-generated content for factual accuracy before sharing

Community Etiquette

  • Be Respectful: Treat all community members with kindness and respect
  • Stay On Topic: Keep discussions relevant to AI, technology, and collaboration
  • Give Credit: Acknowledge sources and collaborators appropriately
  • Help Others: Share knowledge and assist newcomers when possible
  • Follow Guidelines: Adhere to our community guidelines and terms of service
